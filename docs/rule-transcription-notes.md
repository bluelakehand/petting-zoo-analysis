## Visible Card Anatomy

- Top: die roll range needed to move to the card.
- Left/icon row: card type/kind.
- Shield: victory point value.
- Bottom-right coin: purchase cost.
- Text box: card ability.

## Deck Counts And Spatial Terms

- Normal cards: 5 copies each.
- Starting cards: 3 copies each, one for each player in a 3-player game.
- Scoring/victory-point cards: 3 copies each, one per player.
- Victory condition: a player wins by owning one each of the four VP cards:
  Gift Shop, Rooster, Dolphin, and Kangaroo.
- A player cannot own duplicates of a VP card in normal play because there are
  only enough copies for one of each per player.
- Apple Picking is unique: it is not 5 identical copies. There are five
  one-copy variants with rolls `1 or 6`, `2 or 6`, `3 or 6`, `4 or 6`, and
  `5 or 6`.
- Bunny is unique: it is not 5 identical copies. There are five one-copy
  variants with rolls `1`, `2`, `3`, `4`, and `5`.
- Adjacent means touching sides only, so at most 4 spaces.
- Diagonal means touching corners only, so at most 4 spaces.
- Surrounding means touching either a side or corner, so at most 8 spaces.
